Rocket Pool has distributed lrETH (locked rETH), which can be unlocked by connecting your wallet and going through the swap process. The total unlockable lrETH has a market value of 4,8 million dollars. lrETH has been distributed into selected wallets who have directly or indirectly operated with one of Rocket Pools smart contracts.
